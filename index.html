Notepad is a simple text editor program primarily used for creating and editing plain text files. It's a basic utility software that comes pre-installed with Microsoft Windows operating systems. Notepad offers a minimalist interface with limited formatting options, making it suitable for quick note-taking, coding, or editing configuration files. Despite its simplicity, it serves as a versatile tool for various tasks, including writing scripts, jotting down ideas, or drafting simple documents. While lacking advanced features found in more sophisticated text editors, Notepad remains a popular choice for its lightweight design and ease of use.
